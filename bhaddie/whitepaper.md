## 3. The Bhaddie Protocol

### 3.1 Radar Layer

The Radar is a real-time proximity map that displays anonymized "blips" of nearby users. Each blip is color-coded by vibe classification:

| Color | Vibe | Signal |
|-------|------|--------|
| Pink (#ff2d78) | Gym | Post-workout glow detected |
| Violet (#8b5cf6) | Alt | Non-conformist energy radiating |
| Cyan (#06b6d4) | Artsy | Creative aura palpable |
| Amber (#f59e0b) | Downtown | Main character energy active |
| Green (#10b981) | Night Owl | After-hours mystique engaged |

Users cannot see exact locations until a mutual match occurs (Match-Before-Reveal, or MBR). This prevents the obvious dystopian implications while preserving the dopamine loop.

### 3.2 Beacon Layer

The Beacon is an opt-in location broadcasting system with three privacy tiers:

- **Exact Pin**: GPS-precise location. Premium tier. Costs followers 50 tokens to unlock.
- **Fuzzy 500m**: Approximate circle. Standard tier. Costs 20 tokens.
- **District Only**: Neighbourhood name. Free tier. Like saying "I'm in Gastown" without saying where in Gastown.

The privacy tier selection is itself a signal. Choosing "Exact Pin" communicates supreme confidence. Choosing "District Only" communicates mysterious unavailability. Both are valid baddie strategies.

### 3.3 Economy Layer

The Bhaddie Economy is powered by $BHAD tokens (pronounced "bad"):

- **Earning**: Location drops, streak bonuses, follower density milestones, Baddie Hours broadcasting
- **Spending**: Unlocking proximity data, boosting your blip visibility, purchasing verification badges
- **Conversion**: 1 $BHAD = $0.02 USD (subject to vibes-based market conditions)

The conversion rate is pegged to the Vibes Index (VI), a proprietary metric combining:
- Average follower unlock rate
- Streak consistency
- Clout score trajectory
- Time spent in premium broadcast mode

### 3.4 Clout Score

Every user has a Clout Score, calculated as:

```
C = (F * 0.3) + (U * 0.25) + (S * 0.2) + (D * 0.15) + (V * 0.1)

Where:
  F = follower count (normalized)
  U = unlock rate (how often people pay to see your location)
  S = streak length (consecutive broadcast days)
  D = density score (follower concentration within 500m)
  V = verification multiplier (1.0 or 1.5x if verified)
```

The Clout Score is publicly visible and cannot be hidden. This is by design. In the attention economy, your score IS the product.

## 4. Baddie Hours

Baddie Hours are premium time slots when a user commits to broadcasting. Think of them as office hours, but for being perceived. During Baddie Hours:

The optimal Baddie Hours strategy, according to our internal modelling, is 6pm-12am on weekdays and 10am-2am on weekends. These windows maximize the Perception-Per-Hour (PPH) metric.
