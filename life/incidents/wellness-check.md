## Legal Grounds (from case-brief.md)

- **s.8 Charter** — Unlawful warrantless entry into private dwelling. Feeney warrant required. No consent, no exigent circumstances. *(R. v. Feeney [1997] 2 SCR 13)*
- **s.9 Charter** — Arbitrary detention. Wellness call provides no authority to arrest. Walking away is not grounds for arrest.
- **s.7 Charter** — Excessive force (knelt on back, couldn't breathe). Bodily autonomy violated by forced medication. Solitary confinement.
- **s.10(b) Charter** — Denial of right to counsel. No caution administered. No access to counsel. Held overnight.
- **s.12 Charter** — Overnight solitary during acute mental health crisis = cruel and unusual treatment.

**Estimated damages range (BC precedent): $200,000–$600,000**

Key comparator: *Degen v. Ministry of Public Safety* (2023 BCSC) — RCMP excessive force + PTSD → $317k awarded.
